Non-professional drone market is completely dominated by DJI; only professional drones are made in US. So, *which* DJI?

For serious users:
- Mini 5 Pro $770 at DJI; $760 with controller at Amazon; too advanced; with "plus" battery - too heavy
- *Mini 4 Pro* $650 out of stock at DJI; $294 bundle at Amazon
- Mini 3 Pro $500  too dated

Dedicated FPV:
- Avata 360 (2026): specialized, heavier, less agile; requires registration because of weight?
- *Avata 2* (2024): $1290 at Amazon with everything including Goggles 3

For beginners:
- Flip (2025): better camera; no FPV option?
- *Neo 2* (2026): $260 at DJI; better safety and tracking; FPV an option.

Controllers:
- RC N3: uses phone
- RC 2: own screen
- RC 2 Pro: bigger screen
- Motion: gestures

DJI Goggles:
- 3: $500; older, slimmer, dual OLED, PIP
- 3N: $230; mewer, boxier (can be worn over prescription glasses), single LCD, no PIP
