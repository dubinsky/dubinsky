Notes on building Prusa Core One kit (that we bought in 2025 and which became Core One+ before we even started building it in 2026):
- rear screw hole of the left side of the bottom panel has malformed threads (I do not recall destroying them); I used one M3x8rT screw and one M3 nut from the fasteners supplied with in the kit to attach a left rear vertical profile to the bottom panel - and hope that those were the spares ;)
- front left trapezoidal nut did not thread as easily as the rear one;
- I over-tightened a screw on the belt tensioner left and cracked it slightly;
